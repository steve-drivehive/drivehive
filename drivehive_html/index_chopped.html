WHAT IS DRIVEHIVE?

DriveHive provides fans and celebrities the opportunity to work together to raise money for various charities. Fans can pool money together to incentivize celebrities to participate in an exciting, exclusive, live event. If the targeted pledge is not met, the donors won't get charged and nothing will be produced.
